What’s New in ArtOS Antelope Canyon

ArtOS Antelope Canyon provide a new way to work, play or do what want. ArtOS integrate Windows 10 to use your Windows favourite apps

Read the Changes

Work Or Play.

Stay productive with Multitasking View, Multi-Desk, Do Not Disturb, and more.

Multitasking View

Workspaces help organize your work by task. Keep work and play separate, but just one swipe or tap away.

Multi-Desk

ArtOS Multi-Desk provide a new way to organize your files and apps. You can add 2 "Desktops" for each users, each mode of Multi-Desk provide new defaults app, options and many more

Picture-in-Picture

You want to work and also watch a video? Thanks to the Picture-In-Picture you can. When you want, hover over the PiP icon on the video to activate it.

Do Not Disturb

Do Not Disturb organize your notifications for you. Set application notifications to keep only important notifications

Inclued Apps, Without the ones you don't want.

ArtOS include these apps, You can uninstall these, we hate bloatware. You can see apps screenshoot and features.

Photos

Videos

MakeAnApp

Virtualizer
